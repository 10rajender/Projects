
Add item to todo

. You can the item to todo by entering the text in input field.

. Press Add button to add to todo list.


Update Item 

. To update any item in totos press Edit button of corresponding list item.

. The text will appear in the input field to update the item.

. Then Add button will change to update button.

. Press Update button to update the item to todos.



Delete Item

. To delete the item from todos press Delete Button.

. It will delete the corresponding todo item


Clear the Todos

. To clear the multiple items from the todos check the items.

. Press Clear completed button.

. It will clear all the checked items from the Todos.
